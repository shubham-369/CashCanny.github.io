Can recycling cut unsafe migration from Haiti?

2 December 2021

The initiative, launched by the UN's International Organization for Migration (IOM), will not only help to improve the environment, it may also provide new housing which is resilient to natural disasters.On the International Day of Migrants, celebrated annually on 18 December, UN Haiti looks at how recycling could make a contribution towards people staying at home rather than risking migration through unofficial or irregular channels.
